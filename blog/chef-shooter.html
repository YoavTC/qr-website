Chef Shooter Game


Chef Shooter is my firsst completed project in Unity, I made it from my already existing knowledge of C#, and some YouTube tutorials.

The game is a simple 2D wave-shooter, where you play as a chef that needs to protect his kitchen from incoming rats.
The rats vary in size and speed, and the more you kill the faster they spawn and thus it gets more and more difficult.

The game was made using the Unity game engine, design was made with Aseprite and Photoshop.

I made the visual art for the game, while a friend of mine Kuchy made the soundtrack.
